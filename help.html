Save the Coral Reefs

Our coral reefs are decreasing at an alarming rate as global stresses compound into the increasing degredation of our acquatic environment. Corals reefs are herald as the "rain forests of the see" that have over 4000 species if biodiverse acquatic life, but make up less than 1 % of the ocean. According to Reefs at Risk Revisted over 75% of the world's coral reefs are in danger. If we continue with the current state of affairs towards coral reefs, by 2030, 90 percent of all reefs will be in dangers, and by 2050 all of them will be.
